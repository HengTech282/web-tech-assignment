IT Support Technician

Details:

An IT Support Technician helps computer users by communicating through different platforms, including over the phone and face-to-face meetings.
IT Support Technicians solve hardware and software problems and fix issues that may interfere with everyday computer use.
Here are some of their roles and skills:

Salary: $70,000 to $85,000 per year

Fundamental Languages: C++, Python, Java, C#, SQL

Years of Experience (Recommended): 1-4 Years

Responsiblities:

  1. Identifying hardware and software problems
  2. Installing hardware and software
  3. Troubleshooting
  4. Updating computer software and hardware

Skills:

  • Knowledge of computer operating systems, hardware and software (Essential)
  • Customer service skills (Essential)
  • Analytical thinking skills (Preferable)
  • Problem solving skills (Preferable)

Web Designer

Details:

Being a web designer means becoming responsible for not creating the design and layout of a webpage or website.
Their main goal is to focus on the visual aspects of the site such as the fonts and additional aspects such as images.
Here are some of their roles and skills:

Salary: $80,000 to $85,000 per year

Fundamental Languages: HTML, CSS, Python, PHP, C#

Years of Experience (Recommended): 0-2 Years

Key Responsiblities:

  1. Designing webpage templates
  2. Testing and editing websites
  3. Customise web-based applications
  4. Programming new features and maintaining existing ones on a website

Skills:

  • Knowledge of computer operating systems, hardware and software (Essential)
  • Abilty to write computer programs (Essential)
  • Attention to detail (Preferable)
  • Knowledge of Mathematics (Preferable)
